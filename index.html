About us
World Accord MUN, established in January 2024, is a pioneering Model United Nations platform based in Indore. Rooted in a commitment to fostering global awareness and diplomatic skills, our brand envisions expanding its reach beyond Indore to include other major cities. As we embark on this journey, our goal is to create a dynamic and inclusive MUN experience that transcends geographical boundaries, connecting aspiring diplomats and leaders from diverse backgrounds. Stay tuned as we work towards making World Accord MUN a hallmark of excellence in Model United Nations conferences across the nation.
